How to Calculate?

The TOEFL iBT total score is the sum of the scores you receive from the four sections (Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing). Each section is scored from 0 to 30.

Reading and Listening You get an estimated score by entering the number of correct answers for each section. The value and scoring of questions in these sections may vary slightly depending on the overall difficulty of the test and ETS&#39; adjustments.

Speaking and Writing The sections are scored directly on a 0-30 scale by more than one evaluator, based on specific criteria (content, language use, fluency, vocabulary, etc.). You must enter these scores directly.

Your actual TOEFL iBT score is the final score listed on your official scorecard from ETS.

Information About TOEFL iBT

**TOEFL iBT (Internet-Based Test)** is one of the most widely accepted tests for measuring academic English proficiency by English-speaking universities, colleges and institutions around the world. The test assesses your ability to read, listen, speak and write in English in an academic setting.

TOEFL iBT Sections and Durations (Current - After July 2023):

  • **Reading:** ~35 minutes (2 reading passages, 20 questions)
  • **Listening:** ~36 minutes (3 lectures, 2 conversations, 28-39 questions)
  • **Speaking:** 16 minutes (4 tasks)
  • **Writing:** 29 minutes (2 tasks)

The total duration of the exam is approximately **2 hours**. Each section is scored from 0-30 and the total score is from 0-120. Universities and institutions usually set a certain overall and/or section score threshold for admission.

This information is about the general structure of the TOEFL iBT. For the most up-to-date test dates, registration processes, content changes and detailed information, please visit the official ETS TOEFL website.

What is TOEFL iBT and Why is it Important?

**TOEFL iBT** is one of the most internationally recognized ways to prove your English language skills. It is accepted by thousands of universities and institutions, especially in the USA, Canada, UK, Australia and New Zealand. The exam measures your reading, listening, speaking and writing skills in English in an academic environment, showing how prepared you are for the linguistic challenges you may face while studying in a foreign language.

A TOEFL score may be required not only for university admissions but also for some visa applications and professional certifications. A good TOEFL score is a critical step towards achieving your academic and professional goals.

TOEFL iBT Scoring Scale:

  • Each section (Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing) is scored between 0-30.
  • The total score is the sum of the four section scores and ranges from 0 to 120.

How to Prepare for TOEFL iBT?

  • **Official ETS Materials:** Practice with official ETS practice tests and prep books.
  • **Time Management:** Pay special attention to time management during mock exams as each section has a specific time limit.
  • **Note-Taking Skills:** Develop effective note-taking techniques in the Listening and Speaking sections.
  • **Speaking and Writing Practice:** Read out loud, record yourself, write mock essays, and get feedback if possible.
  • **Academic English:** Increase your familiarity with academic language by reading scientific articles and listening to academic podcasts.
