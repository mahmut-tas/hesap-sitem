Privacy Policy

As Hesapkolik.net, we take the privacy and personal data protection of our users seriously. This privacy policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website and use our calculators, how we use and protect this information.

Information We Collect

We generally do not **collect** any personally identifiable information when you visit Hesapkolik.net or use our calculators. Our website takes input directly from you to perform your calculations and displays the results to you instantly. These inputs are not **permanently stored** on our servers.

However, we may collect certain **anonymous and aggregated data** for the purpose of improving overall site performance and user experience:

  • Technical Data: Technical information such as your IP address (anonymized), browser type, operating system, pages visited, site access times may be collected automatically to analyze site traffic and troubleshoot technical problems. This data does not reveal your personal identity.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics, such as which calculators are used the most and which fields are accessed the most on the calculators, may be used to improve site content and functionality. This data is not associated with personal information.

How We Use Information

We use the anonymous and aggregated data we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To improve the performance and user experience of our website.
  • To understand which calculators are popular and guide future content development.
  • Identify and resolve technical issues.
  • To ensure the security of our website.

We never share, sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

Cookies

Hesapkolik.net may use **cookies** to improve user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your computer by your browser. The cookies we use generally include:

  • Session Cookies: They are stored temporarily while you navigate the site and deleted when you close your browser. These cookies are essential for site functionality.
  • Analysis Cookies: Contains anonymous usage data collected through third-party services such as Google Analytics. These cookies help us understand how our website is used and do not collect your personal information.

You have the option to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may result in some features of our website not functioning properly.
